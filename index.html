Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

We do not collect any personal data.

DrinkCraft works entirely on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking — ever.

01

Overview

DrinkCraft ("the App") is a cocktail and coffee recipe application developed by Enver Yavuz. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

02

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any form of account or profile data
03

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party:

  • Your selected language preference
  • Your favorite recipes
  • Your shopping list items
  • Your light/dark theme preference

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser or app cache.

04

Third-Party Services

DrinkCraft does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any external parties.

05

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13. The App is a recipe reference tool and does not require any personal information to function.

06

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is ever updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Since we collect no data, changes are likely to be minimal.
